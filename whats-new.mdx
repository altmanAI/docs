## Everything Shipped in v2.0

Every feature below is live and available in your DailyPilot account today. Click any card to read the full documentation for that feature.

P.A.I.H.I. ensures that every recommendation, reflection, and progress summary is grounded in real evidence — not hype, not hallucination.
