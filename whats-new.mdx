## The v2.0 Standard

Every feature in DailyPilot v2.0 was evaluated against three criteria before shipping: **Human Control**, **Transparency**, and **Measurable Impact**. Human Control means you retain decision authority at every step. Transparency means the system is auditable and honest about what it does and does not know. Measurable Impact means features must produce real, observable value in your daily life — not just engagement metrics.
