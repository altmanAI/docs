DailyPilot is a human-first AI productivity co-pilot built by AltmanAI. This page introduces what DailyPilot is, explains the philosophy behind it, and shows you how every feature fits together to help you run each day with intention — so you always know what you're doing, why it matters, and what comes next.

## What Is DailyPilot?

DailyPilot is a web app designed for intentional daily execution. It helps you turn ambition into action by giving you a structured place to plan your day, track real progress, reflect with honesty, and stay aligned with the goals and values that actually matter to you.

Most AI tools are built around the premise that intelligence should replace human judgment. DailyPilot is built on the opposite belief: **Humanity leads. Intelligence follows.** Pilot AI, the app's built-in co-pilot, is there to support your thinking — not substitute it. You stay in the driver's seat. The AI helps you see the road more clearly.

Every feature inside DailyPilot flows from one core question: *Are you living and working in a way that reflects what you actually care about?* Whether that's a long-term goal, a personal value, or simply finishing today's most important task, DailyPilot keeps that question front and center.

## Why DailyPilot Is Different

Generic AI tools are optimized for output: write faster, search faster, generate more. DailyPilot is optimized for *judgment* — helping you decide what to work on, understand why it matters, and act in ways that are consistent with who you want to become.

Here is what sets DailyPilot apart:

- **Human judgment leads every session.** Pilot AI never takes the wheel. It clarifies, suggests, and reflects — but every decision is yours.
- **The P.A.I.H.I. Operating System** grounds every insight in real evidence through five principles: Proof, Alignment, Integrity, Humanity, and Impact. No hype, no hallucination.
- **Progress is auditable.** The Impact Ledger creates a transparent record of your actual trajectory, so you can see growth over days, weeks, and months.
- **Privacy is built in.** The Trust Center gives you full control over your data, audit logs, and privacy settings from day one.
- **It works across your whole life.** DailyPilot is not a task manager or a note-taking app. It is a complete daily operating system that connects your actions, goals, and values in one place.
