DailyPilot lives at [altmanai.tech](https://www.altmanai.tech) — the official home of AltmanAI's products. Getting started takes just a few steps: create a free account, choose how you'd like to sign in, and walk through a short onboarding flow that helps DailyPilot understand your goals and daily rhythm. No credit card is required, and you can be inside your dashboard in under two minutes.

## Creating your account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the registration page">
    Open [https://www.altmanai.tech/register](https://www.altmanai.tech/register) in any modern browser.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your sign-in method">
    You have two options:

    - **Continue with Google** — recommended for the fastest setup. One click connects your Google account securely, no extra password needed.
    - **Email and password** — enter your email address, create a password, and confirm it in the field below.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Create account'">
    Hit the **Create account** button to submit your details and create your profile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the onboarding flow">
    DailyPilot will walk you through a short setup sequence. You'll define your first goal, identify the personal values that matter most to you, and set your daily rhythm preferences so the app can surface check-ins and prompts at the right moments.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You're in">
    Your DailyPilot dashboard is ready. From here you can start a check-in, set milestones, explore the Impact Ledger, or chat with your Pilot AI — all from the same screen.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Logging in

Once your account exists, return to [https://www.altmanai.tech/login](https://www.altmanai.tech/login) any time. The login screen offers the same two options you used at signup — **Continue with Google** or your **email and password** combination. Choose whichever method you registered with.

## Resetting your password

If you signed up with email and password and can't remember your credentials:

1. On the login screen, click **Forgot password?** below the password field.
2. Enter the email address associated with your account and click **Send reset link**.
3. Check your inbox for a reset link from DailyPilot and follow the instructions to set a new password.

If you signed up with Google SSO, password reset doesn't apply — use the **Continue with Google** button instead.

<Note>
  DailyPilot is a web app, so you can access it from any modern browser — Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge, and others — on your phone, tablet, laptop, or desktop. No installation required.
</Note>

## Account FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need a credit card to sign up?">

  </Accordion>
