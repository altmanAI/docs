DailyPilot is built around you — your goals, your reflections, your daily momentum. Because the product is so personal, how your data is handled matters. DailyPilot stores only what's needed to make your experience work, never sells or shares your information, and gives you clear controls to manage, export, or delete your data whenever you choose.

## Your data belongs to you

When you use DailyPilot, the information you create — check-ins, goals, reflections, and logged actions — lives in your account and powers your experience. It is yours. AltmanAI does not claim ownership of your personal data, and you can request its export or remove it at any time through your account settings.

## What data DailyPilot stores

DailyPilot stores the following categories of information to deliver a personalised and coherent experience across sessions:

- **Check-in entries and responses** — your answers to daily and contextual check-in prompts
- **Goal and milestone data** — the goals you set, milestones you track, and progress you record
- **Reflection journal entries** — written reflections you add to your journal
- **Impact Ledger actions** — the actions and contributions you log in your Impact Ledger
- **Pilot AI conversation history** — your conversations with Pilot AI, used to keep responses relevant and contextually aware over time

## What DailyPilot does NOT do

DailyPilot does not:

- Sell your personal data to third parties
- Use your data to serve you advertisements
- Share your information with third parties without your consent
- Use your content to train general AI models outside your own personalised experience

## How Pilot AI uses your data

Pilot AI — DailyPilot's built-in AI assistant — uses your stored data solely to personalise responses within your DailyPilot account. When you ask Pilot a question or request a check-in summary, it draws on your history to give you a more relevant, grounded answer. That context stays within your account and is not used outside of it.

## Your controls

You are in control of your data at every stage:

- **Account settings** — update your profile, preferences, and connected accounts at any time
- **Data export** — request a copy of your data through your account settings
- **Account deletion** — permanently delete your account and associated data when you choose to leave
