# Transparent product development under the official AltmanAI domain.

DailyPilot is an official AltmanAI product currently hosted on AltmanAI.tech. The domain serves as the company’s public brand home and active web app distribution platform.

Domain Integrity

## Why this domain is used.

AltmanAI.tech is the official company domain for AltmanAI. Hosting DailyPilot here gives users a direct connection to the company behind the product. It also avoids unnecessary dependency on native app marketplaces during early-stage development.

The decision is intentional: the web app can be improved quickly, accessed broadly, and distributed directly while AltmanAI continues refining the product experience.

Development Status
