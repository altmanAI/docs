# DailyPilot

DailyPilot is an intentional, human-first productivity co-pilot for planning the day, tracking progress, reflecting with clarity, and aligning action with long-term goals.

[Launch DailyPilot](https://www.altmanai.tech/launch) [Why it lives on AltmanAI.tech](https://www.altmanai.tech/trust-center)

Purpose

## Designed for intentional daily execution.

### Plan

Turn the day into a focused operating plan with priorities, commitments, and meaningful action.

### Track

Log progress, capture wins, and make daily momentum visible.

### Reflect

Use structured reflection to learn from the day instead of simply moving through it.

### Align

Connect daily behavior with long-term goals, identity, values, and personal direction.

Distribution

## A web app first, by design.

DailyPilot is currently distributed as a web app on AltmanAI.tech. This allows AltmanAI to improve the product quickly, ship updates without app marketplace delays, and keep the experience directly connected to the company’s official digital infrastructure.

Native app marketplace distribution may come later. For now, the web app model gives DailyPilot the flexibility it needs during active development while still allowing users to access the product from phones, tablets, laptops, and desktops.
