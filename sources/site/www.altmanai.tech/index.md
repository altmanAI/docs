Official Distribution Strategy

### Why DailyPilot lives on AltmanAI.tech

DailyPilot is an official product of AltmanAI. Hosting it on the AltmanAI.tech domain makes the relationship clear: the company owns the platform, the product, and the development environment.

Web app distribution allows AltmanAI to build faster, ship updates immediately, reduce platform dependency, and validate the product directly with users before expanding into native app marketplaces.

This approach keeps DailyPilot accessible from any modern browser while preserving the option to release on the Apple App Store or other app marketplaces when the product is ready for that stage.

In short: AltmanAI.tech is not a placeholder. It is the official home of AltmanAI’s product ecosystem and the current distribution platform for DailyPilot.
