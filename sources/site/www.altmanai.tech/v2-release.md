## What is DailyPilot?

DailyPilot is an AI co-pilot that helps you run your day with intention — plan what matters, track real progress, reflect honestly, and stay aligned with the life you actually want. It's built on one belief: Humanity leads. Intelligence follows.

Most AI tools are built to replace your judgment. DailyPilot is built to return it to you. By the time you finish your first day inside the app, you'll know exactly what you're doing, why it matters, and what to do next.
