DailyPilot is built to be useful from the very first session. This guide walks you through everything you need to go from a brand-new account to completing your first full day of intentional planning, AI-assisted decision-making, and honest reflection — all in about five minutes of setup.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Go to [https://www.altmanai.tech/register](https://www.altmanai.tech/register) and sign up using your Google account or an email address and password. No credit card is required — your account is active the moment you complete registration.

    Once your account is created, you land directly in the DailyPilot onboarding flow.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete onboarding">
    DailyPilot walks you through a short onboarding sequence designed to make your first day meaningful, not generic. You will:

    - **Set your first goal** — name something you are actively working toward.
    - **Define your personal values** — articulate what matters most to you so DailyPilot can help you stay aligned.
    - **Choose your daily rhythm preferences** — tell DailyPilot when you like to plan, how you prefer to reflect, and what a good day looks like for you.

    Onboarding takes most users under three minutes. You can update every setting later from your profile.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start your morning check-in">
    Your morning check-in is where each day begins. Open DailyPilot in the morning and set your intentions for the day:

    - **Priorities** — the two or three things that genuinely matter today.
    - **Commitments** — the specific actions you are committing to complete.
    - **The one thing** — the single most important outcome you want to achieve before the day ends.

    The check-in takes two to five minutes and gives you a clear operating plan before you open your first email or meeting.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Let Pilot AI assist">
    Pilot AI is your built-in AI co-pilot, available throughout the day. After your morning check-in, try asking Pilot AI to help you with one of these:

    - **Clarify a decision** — describe a choice you are wrestling with and let Pilot AI help you think it through.
    - **Break down a goal** — take a big ambition and have Pilot AI help you decompose it into concrete next actions.
    - **Suggest your next action** — if you feel stuck or scattered, ask Pilot AI what to focus on next based on your current priorities.

    Pilot AI supports your thinking — it does not replace it. Every suggestion is yours to accept, adjust, or discard.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete your evening reflection">
    Close the day with an honest debrief. Your evening reflection prompts you to answer three essential questions:

    1. **What happened?** — A brief, factual account of the day.
    2. **What did you learn?** — Anything worth carrying forward: about your work, your decisions, or yourself.
    3. **What's next?** — The one thing you want to prioritize when tomorrow begins.

    Each completed reflection is saved to your Impact Ledger, building a transparent record of your real trajectory over time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  DailyPilot is a web app — no installation needed. Access it from your phone, tablet, laptop, or desktop by visiting [https://www.altmanai.tech/launch](https://www.altmanai.tech/launch) in any modern browser. Bookmark the link for instant access at the start and end of every day.
</Tip>
