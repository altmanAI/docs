## How DailyPilot Works

DailyPilot structures your day around four core actions — Plan, Track, Reflect, and Align. Each one builds on the last, creating a compounding loop of clarity and intentional progress.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up free at [altmanai.tech/register](https://www.altmanai.tech/register) using Google or email. No credit card required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your intentions">
    Each morning, open DailyPilot and capture your priorities, commitments, and the one thing that matters most today.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track your progress">
    Log wins, capture momentum, and let Pilot AI help you break through blockers throughout the day.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reflect and align">
    Each evening, complete your daily reflection. Over time, these compound into real self-knowledge and measurable impact.
  </Step>
</Steps>
